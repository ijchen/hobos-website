Hobo Clan

The Hobos are a competitive e-sports organization primarily focused on Overwatch. They have been growing the team and participating in competitive games since early season 29, and now have over half a dozen members.

Some of the core principles of the Hobo Clan are to enjoy the game, learn from your mistakes and improve your gameplay, and to work together to achieve your goals.
