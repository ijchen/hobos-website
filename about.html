About Hobos

We are Hobos, a competitive e-sports organization primarily focused on Overwatch. We have been growing the team and participating in competitive games since early season 29, and now have over half a dozen members.

We focus on learning and improving with a growth mindset. We encourage our members to enjoy the game, and work actively seek to work together effectively.

Hobos is currently run by Bue, who founded the clan and has been managing it ever since.

Our team communicates primarily in a private Discord server. We regularly practice in quick-play games to warm up, then hop into competitive matches after a few practice games. We don't play on a regular schedule, since the team member's individual schedules would be too difficult to permanently coordinate.

For information about our roster, visit the roster page!
